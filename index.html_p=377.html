Ariana Persian Text To Speech

Ariana provides high quality Persian text to speech service. You can use Ariana Farsi speech synthesis in your applications either by installing on-premise Ariana service package on your own server or by creating and charging your account and connecting to our cloud TTS servers.

You can listen to our natural sounding TTS voices using Ariana Telegram Bot (robot) or the online text to voice demo:

Features

  • Supports both RESTful and WebSocket web services
  • High voice quality and accuracy
  • Fast response in concurrent and lengthy requests
  • Supports user-defined words
  • Reads English words, diacritics, symbols and punctuations
  • Caches most frequent requests
  • Customizable pitch, speed and gain
  • Supports various audio formats (wave, mp3, ogg:opus)
